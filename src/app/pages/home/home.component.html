Welcome to the First European LGBTQIA+ Convention of Narcotics Anonymous

Join us in Paris from June 27th to 29th, 2025, for the First European LGBTQIA+ Convention of Narcotics Anonymous organized by the LGBTQIA+ Committee of the French region of NA. An event celebrating unity, diversity, and recovery within the Narcotics Anonymous community

This convention is an opportunity to connect, share experiences, and strengthen our fellowship and recovery through a weekend of meetings, events, and celebrations.

Why Did We Decide to Organize This Convention?

In August 2021, a group of addicts, supported by the local GSRs, came together to establish an LGBTQIA+ subcommittee within the Central Paris Area. Three months later, this collective effort led to the first-ever LGBTQIA+ convention in the French region, bringing addicts together from all over the country. This event marked the beginning of a service journey that, over the next three years, included four successful conventions, an H&I meeting, and numerous PI initiatives.

Last year, these efforts culminated in the passing of a motion to create an LGBTQIA+ committee to serve the whole French region, with the purpose of carrying the message of Narcotics Anonymous to the LGBTQIA+ addict who still suffers and helping all addicts feel included in our life-saving fellowship.

Now, for its fifth edition, the French LGBTQIA+ convention is expanding to a European scale. This year, from June 27th to 29th, 2025, we invite addicts from across the globe to join us in celebrating the unity and diversity of our beautiful fellowship.
